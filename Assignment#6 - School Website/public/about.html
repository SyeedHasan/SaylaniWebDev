Why Us?


At Hasans School of Law legal education is personalized to each of one of our students. Academically, Hasan School of Law is pragmatic and innovative in its approach to legal education. Our distinguishing factor, however, is our focus and emphasis on law in practice and associated legal skills. We strive to ensure that our students are equipped with practical legal skills and are able to apply their understanding of legal theory in practice. When you study at Hasans School of Law, you will have access to legal education led by the best law faculty in the city with great study facilities, including an extensive collections of books and learning resources to support your studies and wireless access throughout the campus. Some of the salient points of Hasans School of Law include:
Outstanding Teachhing and Results
As per the University of London’s Institution Annual Monitoring 2015-2016, Hasans School of Law’s LLB (Hons) students in the May/June Examination Session of 2015-16 achieved a record breaking and unprecedented passing rate of 85%, and our results were ranked as 1st in Karachi and one of the best nationwide amongst all University of London law programme providers.
Legal Skills Course:
Hasans School of Law is proud to pioneer and provide the ‘Legal Skills Course’, a module based foundational certificate course designed in collaboration with prominent lawyers which will provide our students with a firm grounding in and essential knowledge of the local laws and procedures. We are the only law school in Karachi which is offering such vocational legal skills course in a formal modular manner along with University of London’s LLB (Hons) Programme.
Professional Development Center
A dedicated department within Hasans School of Law which is focused on professional legal training and development of working professionals from a wide array of organizations and industries by organizing various training courses and workshops in the areas of law and regulatory compliances. Students and alumni of Hasans School of Law can attend the said workshops/trainings at an economical rate.

Additional Benefits of studying the LLB (Hons.) at Hasans School of Law, Karachi

Hasans School of Law is managed by a team of professional lawyers. This ensures that the highest standard of legal education is maintained and imparted to the students. Hasans School of Law has a competitive fee structure, compared to other law schools. We ensure that our students get the best value at the most competitive cost. Teaching of the highest quality from a team of experienced professors and lecturers, each of whom have practiced/practices as an Advocate and/or served as a Partner of a renowned law firm. Highest level of student satisfaction rate across all the University of London’s law programme providers in Karachi. Recognition of excellence by the University of London. Hasans School of Law is the only law school in Karachi which allows students to enroll for a minimum of 2 subjects of their choice. So you can enroll in Hasans School of Law for only two subjects of your choice or for the entire course load of 4 subjects*.
