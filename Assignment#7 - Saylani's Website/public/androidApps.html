Saylani Welfare Offical Application

Through this mobile application we intend to connect with those who are always on the go and wants to know what’s the current update of our efforts. This application focuses on communicating all information about Saylani welfare services in the society and also allows you to become a participant in it.

 

Tafseer-e-Quran Application

Tafseer means explanation of Quran. The best way to explain any Quranic Verse is to support it first with various other related Quranic Verses. Afterwards, Hadith is used for strengthening such an explanation or tafseer. This Application has been developed to facilitate the reading of very popular Tafseer by “Mufti Asif Abdullah ” in Urdu language.

 

Al-Hajj Application

The Al Hajj Application provides comprehensive information related to Hajj in one compact package!… It’s easy to navigate “one page fit” interface provides a step-by-step description of all Hajj related matters and takes away the agony of flipping through various pages to get you through directly to the information that you really want! .This app also includes a “Tasbeeh Counter”.

 

Daily Momin Application

We have been sent to earth for a purpose. Today all the muslims are occupied fully with zillions of activities to become successful in this world. We have forgotten that our core objective in this world is to prepare for the rozah hashar and day of judgement. Since we are sent to the world we do have worldy obligations but our objective should still be clear to prepare for jannah.In this busy world we need to use technology to help us remind our basic religious obligations to be a momin.
